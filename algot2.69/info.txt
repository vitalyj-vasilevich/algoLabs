The task is called water lilies.
There is a long line-shaped pond, with water lilies growing in it.
There is a particular amount of mosquitoes on an every water lily.
Input has number of lilies and weights of all of them.
Frog can jump over one or two lilies only. When the frog is on the lily, it eats all the mosquitoes on it. 
The output should be maximum amount of mosquitoes the frog can eat, or -1 if the frog is unable to reach the last lily.
